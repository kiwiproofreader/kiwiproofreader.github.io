Pricing

Kiwi Proofreader prices are the best value you will find anywhere.

The minimum is NZ $10 for small documents of 500 words or less, or NZ $20 per 1000 words. The weekend rate is $30 per 1000 words (weekend runs from sunset on Friday to sunrise on Monday, as well as public holidays). For longer documents, such as a thesis or book manuscript, there is a 25% reduction in fees and the weekend rate does not apply.

There may be a surcharge for urgent work (to be completed within 12 hours).

Payment is made directly into an online-only account by internet transfer. Details of the account will be emailed to you once your work has been edited by Kiwi Proofreader.
